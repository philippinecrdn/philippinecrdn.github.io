Why am I here?

Here is a fun fact: I never wanted to come to Miami. It has never been in my plan. I wanted to go to New-York. It was and still is, my dream.

When I applied to study abroad I never checked Miami, not even once. So imagine my reaction when I was announced I was coming here. Well you can't really imagine so here is a description:

I was sitting first row in my American History class when I received an email notification "MICEFA Placement and Application Information".

Of course I was not expecting to receive an answer on that day, February 1st 2022. So I jumped on my notification, only to realize I was not going to my dream city nor to California but to Miami.

I stared my screen for several minutes not knowing what to do because of course I was still supposed to be focus on my class so I couldn't really expressed my feelings.

My first thought was: "What I am going to do in Miami? It is absolutely not a city that was made for me". I live in Paris and I fit the French clich√© on several points:

  • I love museums
  • I love French food (such as croissants of course)
  • I am always in a hurry, I need to walk fast

Plus, I don't like hot temperature, I prefer winter (maybe it's because I was born in December). I don't especially like to go to the beach, I don't have my driver's licence and I hate partying (I went to a club once in Italy, I am never going agains). Miami was far from being the right fit.

But against all odds, I accepted the offer (well, I didn't have much choice) and here I am.
